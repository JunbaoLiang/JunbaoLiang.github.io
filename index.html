Selected projects

Four threads that share a method — generate molecular-scale signals, then make them legible.

01 F1 · 0.92

Binding-pocket prediction from protein structure

A grid-based pocket detector and XGBoost classifier built on geometric and pharmacophore features of local neighborhoods.

  • Labeled pocket grid points from known ligand coordinates (≤ 4 Å) and trained on held-out chains.
  • Scaled inference with cKDTree spatial indexing and batched 3D grids.
  • Exported probability-scored pocket points as PDB for visualization in PyMOL.

02 Protein Sci · 2025

Glycosylated DszB — reducing product inhibition

Artificial β-glucose decoration of a desulfinase enzyme to disrupt the product-trapping loop dynamics that throttle catalysis.

  • AMOEBA MD on glycosylated and apo variants; characterized loop dynamics with RMSD / RMSF.
  • Ramachandran analysis connected glycation sites to reduced product trapping.
  • Published in Protein Science; co-first contributions on simulation and analysis.

03 Manuscript · in prep

Sodium-ion electrolyte selection in diglyme

Polarizable and fixed-charge MD across NaPF₆ and NaOTF in diglyme to rationalize experimental transport trends.

  • Simulated 0.6 M / 1 M NaPF₆ and 1 M NaOTF with AMOEBA and CHARMM.
  • Quantified diffusion, RDF, and solvation structure to compare salts.
  • Guided collaborator electrolyte choice for sodium battery prototypes.

04 Active · 2025–

Peptide hydration dynamics across glucose gradients

A benchmark dataset of solvated pentamers and sequence mixtures spanning 0–2 M glucose to quantify composition-dependent hydration shifts.

  • VACF fits for diffusion and retardation; H-bond statistics; RDF, Rg, SASA.
  • Connects sequence composition to glucose-induced dynamical slowdown.
  • Designed to feed downstream ML models on biomolecular hydration.
